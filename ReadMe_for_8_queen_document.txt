1:-)Initial State:
A 8*8 2d-matrix with all entries equal to 0;

2:-)states:
A state is any arrangement of 0 to 8 queens .The index shows the current position of all 1-8 queens
such that no queen is under attack(either row/collumn wise Or diagonally);

3:-)cost:
At every step the cost have the same value i.e. the configuration of the next row queen from the current position; 

4:-)Goal State:
stop at one of the config. such that no one is under attack by any other by
another queen(either row/collumn wise Or diagonally);
